Payment Policy
Deposit Policy
We will process a deposit of at least one night’s stay — or 50% for stays of two or more nights.
Cancellation Policy
With a notice of no less than 14 days, we will be pleased to refund your deposit less a processing fee of RM30.
For reservations cancelled less than 14 days prior, deposit will be refunded less fee provided the room is rebooked.
You are responsible for the entire stay if you fail to show, depart early, or arrive after scheduled dates.
Major holidays, fall foliage, and special events require a 30-day notification.
A Gentle Reminder
01. Buckeye Bed and Breakfast maintains a totally NON-SMOKING environment.
02. As we are an adult romantic getaway, the inn is not suitable for children under 15 years old.
03.Buckeye is wheelchair accessible.
